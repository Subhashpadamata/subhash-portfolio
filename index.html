About Me

I am a Formal Verification Engineer focused on proving RTL design correctness using Formal Verification, Assertion-Based Verification, and SystemVerilog Assertions.

My work includes writing properties, debugging counterexamples, improving proof convergence, and validating protocol behavior using VC Formal, JasperGold, Verdi, and Linux.

I enjoy engineering-focused verification work where clear specifications, strong assertions, and disciplined RTL analysis turn complex design behavior into measurable confidence.
